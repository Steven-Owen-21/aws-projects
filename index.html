About Me

A bit about myself

I've spent 18 years as a General Manager, leading teams, solving complex operational problems, and making decisions under pressure. That career gave me something that's hard to teach — resilience, resourcefulness, and the ability to figure things out independently.

Over the past year I've been learning AWS to build a stock control system from the ground up. What started as a practical project turned into a genuine passion. I found myself going deeper and deeper into cloud architecture, automation, and AI — and realising this is the career I want to build.

I bring an unusual combination to the table: the technical curiosity of someone who fell in love with cloud engineering, and the real-world experience of someone who has managed people, budgets, and operations at scale for nearly two decades. I'm hungry to grow fast and contribute from day one.
